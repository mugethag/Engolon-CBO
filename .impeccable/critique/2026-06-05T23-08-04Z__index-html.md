## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: Not immediately AI-looking — the dark editorial aesthetic is genuine and committed. The warm orange plus near-black plus Playfair Display system does not trigger the "charity blue + stock photo" reflex. The site has a real visual identity. However, three structural patterns give it away at the content level.

**Deterministic scan (3 findings):**
- single-font at line 11: FALSE POSITIVE. The site uses Playfair Display, Lora, and system sans. The detector saw only the Lora link. Disregard.
- em-dash-overuse: CONFIRMED. 33 em-dashes in body text.
- aphoristic-cadence: CONFIRMED. 3 aphoristic constructions: "They don't need much. Just each other." / "One board. One mentor. One child whose life just changed." / "When a mother earns, her whole family eats."

## Overall Impression

The dark editorial aesthetic is the site's biggest asset and should be defended. The visual identity works. The problems are structural: copy patterns and UX scaffolding reveal the AI construction layer beneath the brand. Fix the copy cadence and the nav overload and the site reads like something a real organization built. The donate button going nowhere is the single biggest trust failure.

## What's Working

1. Dark editorial aesthetic is genuine and committed. Near-black with warm orange accent and Playfair Display is a deliberate identity, not an AI default.
2. Program pricing transparency is unusual and powerful. "$5 — sanitary pads, 1 month, 1 girl" is specific and trust-building.
3. The em.accent italic orange heading pattern is consistent and on-brand throughout.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Section eyebrow on every section**
Every section has a .section-label above the h2. This is the AI scaffolding pattern DESIGN.md prohibits. Target: 3-4 maximum on the full page, each earning its place.
Fix: Remove where it restates the obvious. Keep where it adds distinct named information.
Command: /impeccable clarify

**[P1] 33 em-dashes in body copy**
Em-dashes are the strongest AI writing signal in 2026 prose. Replace prose em-dashes with commas, colons, or restructured sentences. The typographic em-dash in the hero location line should stay.
Command: /impeccable clarify

**[P2] Donate button has no payment pathway**
Both donate buttons are placeholder JS. A donor ready to give is blocked. Connect to a real mechanism or show an email/bank transfer path.
Command: /impeccable harden

**[P2] Navigation has 9 items**
Nine top-level items exceed the 5-item working memory threshold. Consolidate to 5-6.
Command: /impeccable layout

**[P2] Testimonials are generic**
Six testimonials with identical structure, no photos, no dates, names from cities not connected to Kibra/Kajiado programs. Replace with 2-3 specific, photographed testimonials.
Command: /impeccable clarify

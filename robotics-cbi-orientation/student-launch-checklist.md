# Student Launch Checklist

Use this checklist to make the orientation usable for students.

## Ready Now

- Full 4:15 HyperFrames orientation composition: `index.html`
- Full narration script: `narration-script.md`
- Voiceover-ready plain text: `voiceover-script.txt`
- Storyboard aligned to production timing: `storyboard.md`
- MVP course map and Term 1 materials: `course-mvp/`

## Before Student Release

1. Review the orientation video in HyperFrames preview.
2. Record or generate narration from `voiceover-script.txt`.
3. Add the audio track to the HyperFrames composition or render the video and combine narration in the final MP4 workflow.
4. Render the orientation to MP4.
5. Place the MP4 in the website, LMS, or course platform.
6. Publish Term 1 Module 1 and Module 2 materials from `course-mvp/term-1/`.
7. Add the simulation lab as the first practical activity after Module 2.

## Minimum Student Sequence

1. Watch Program Orientation.
2. Read Term 1 Course Map.
3. Complete Module 1 lesson, handout, and quiz.
4. Complete Module 2 lesson, handout, and quiz.
5. Complete Simulation Lab 01.
6. Submit lab reflection and evidence.

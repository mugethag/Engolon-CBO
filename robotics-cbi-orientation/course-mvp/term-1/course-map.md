## Term 1 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this MVP term, learners should be able to:

1. Describe robotic subsystems and their functions.
2. Explain the role of sensors, actuators, controllers, and feedback.
3. Apply basic kinematic and dynamic ideas to robot motion.
4. Compare robotic systems across environmental, industrial, civilian, and defense contexts.
5. Explain sustainability robotics and bio-inspired system design.
6. Identify drone components and flight control subsystems.
7. Describe autonomous navigation at an introductory level.
8. Design a basic feedback control response in simulation.
9. Assess simple system behavior using stability and transient response language.

Four-week plan

Built around visible learner outputs.

Every week ends with a small deliverable. The final week asks teams to explain a robot concept tied to a real local problem.

Week 3

Create behavior that reacts to the environment.

Week 4

Applied project

Submit and present a local problem-solving robot concept.

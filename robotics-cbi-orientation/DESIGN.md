## Style Prompt

Create a premium online-learning video identity for Engolon CBO's robotics and autonomous systems certificate. The style should feel serious, technical, and globally credible while retaining the organization's dark editorial warmth: a near-black canvas, warm cream typography, orange accents, and restrained green/teal technical highlights for robotics diagrams, sensor paths, and data flows.

## Colors

- Background: `#0d0d0d`
- Surface: `#161616`
- Warm text: `#f5f0e8`
- Muted text: `rgba(245, 240, 232, 0.68)`
- Engolon orange: `#e8833a`
- Technical green: `#00aa6c`
- Data cyan: `#55c7d9`

## Typography

- Headings: `Playfair Display`, Georgia, serif
- Interface and body text: `Inter`, `Segoe UI`, system sans-serif
- Use large editorial headings only for title and major section scenes. Use compact, high-readability sans-serif text for objectives, module lists, captions, and labels.

## Motion

- Use calm, precise motion: fades, short vertical reveals, timeline sweeps, staggered nodes, and controlled diagram tracing.
- Avoid excessive rotation, bouncing, or playful motion.
- Every scene should end in a stable instructional frame that can be screen-captured for learner notes.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic blue corporate slide styling.
- Do not make the palette one-note orange or one-note green.
- Do not overcrowd scenes with the full curriculum text.
- Do not use decorative blobs, busy gradients, or unreadable tiny labels.
- Do not make the video feel like a marketing landing page; it should feel like a polished learning object.

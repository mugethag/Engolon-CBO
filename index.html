Caring for All, Leaving None Behind

Engolon Community Based Organization serves and uplifts the most vulnerable members of our community: women, children, the elderly, and persons with disabilities.

Our Mission

We exist to serve and uplift the most vulnerable members of our community

"Caring for all, leaving none behind."

Engolon Community Based Organization is a Kenya-centered organization with a global audience, dedicated to empowering vulnerable populations through skills training, financial support, education, and access to essential services. We believe that every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and the tools to improve their lives.

Our Work

Targeted support for the communities we serve

👩

Women

  • Provide skills training and financial literacy
  • Support income-generating activities and entrepreneurship
  • Introduce digital tools for small business management
👶

Children

  • Support school fees, supplies, and mentorship
  • Provide books and learning materials
  • Offer computers and digital literacy programs
  • Advocate for child protection rights
👴

Elderly

  • Offer social support and healthcare access
  • Promote community engagement to reduce isolation
  • Assist with access to government services (e.g., Inua Jamii, NHIF)
♿

Persons with Disabilities (PWDs)

  • Advocate for inclusion and accessibility
  • Ensure equal access to books, computers, and learning resources
  • Provide assistive devices
  • Support communication and daily living

Our Approach to Technology

We believe technology, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), should be a tool – not a distraction. Technology alone cannot solve the challenges facing vulnerable communities. Instead, we view it as one tool among many to achieve our mission.

Our Technology Philosophy: We leverage technology where it adds value in business, learning, accessibility, or access to services. However, our focus remains people, not technology.

How We Use Technology:

  • → Digital Skills Training: Teaching women and youth how to use computers and digital tools for economic opportunity
  • → Accessibility Tools: Assistive devices and software for persons with disabilities to communicate and engage independently
  • → Service Access: Helping elderly and vulnerable populations navigate digital government services and healthcare platforms
  • → Business Tools: Supporting small entrepreneurs with affordable software for managing their businesses
  • → Learning Resources: Providing online and offline educational materials to bridge the digital divide

Technology is a bridge to opportunity, not the destination. Our core commitment is to the people we serve—their dignity, wellbeing, and empowerment.

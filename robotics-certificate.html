Program Overview

From foundations to an applied capstone.

Learners build a foundation in robotics, drone systems, autonomy, AI, swarm coordination, cybersecurity, sustainable robotics, infrastructure applications, and future intelligent systems.

Year 1: Foundational Concepts

  • Core robotics, intelligent systems, drones and control
  • Autonomy, swarms and environmental monitoring
  • AI, cybersecurity and decision-making under uncertainty

Year 2: Advanced Intelligent Systems

  • Infrastructure robotics and smart inspection systems
  • Computer vision, drone perception and quantum concepts
  • Capstone project integrating AI, autonomy, security and sustainability

Learning Outcomes

What learners will be able to do.

Develop expertise in robotics, drones and autonomous systems.
Apply AI and machine learning in robotic environments.
Understand swarm intelligence and coordinated autonomous systems.
Analyze cybersecurity risks in drone and robotic ecosystems.
Explore ethical and technological implications of drone warfare systems.
Introduce quantum computing concepts relevant to robotics and AI.
Design sustainable and secure autonomous systems for real-world challenges.
